Chaliapin Steak Don

The dish is exclusively made by Sōma combined with the Japanese original dish, the Chaliapin Steak, and the donburi concept for his first Shokugeki. Although the dish was made with cheap ingredients that can be found in a supermarket, the meal focused on both innovation and balance of the ingredients to fulfill the donburi mantra, "Dons are completed in one bowl."
